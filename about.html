About Canuck’d Up

Hey, I’m B — a sarcastic, maple-syrup-fuelled Canadian dad who’s seen it all and has opinions on everything. Canuck’d Up is my unfiltered look at life, health, money, and the occasional zombie outbreak.

Each episode, I dive into the absurdities of modern living, share prepping tips, rant about financial pitfalls, and keep it real with a dash of dark Canadian humor.

The unofficial motto: Functional realism in a dysfunctional world.
