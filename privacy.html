Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 13, 2025

Canuck’d Up (“we,” “us,” “our”) is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information when you visit our website canuckdup.ca (the “Site”).

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal Data: Name and email address when you subscribe to our newsletter.
  • Usage Data: Pages you visit, time spent, device/browser type via analytics.
  • Cookies: Small files placed on your device to improve site functionality and analyze traffic.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To send you email updates about new episodes (only if you opt in).
  • To operate, maintain, and improve the Site.
  • To understand how users interact with our content via analytics tools.

3. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data. We may share aggregated, non-identifiable usage data with third-party analytics providers.

4. Cookies & Tracking

You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but some features of the Site may not function properly without them.

5. Third-Party Links

The Site may contain links to third-party websites (e.g., sponsors). We are not responsible for their privacy practices; please review their policies independently.

6. Children’s Privacy

The Site is intended for a general audience. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

7. Your Rights

You may at any time:

  • Request access to your personal data.
  • Request correction or deletion of your personal data.
  • Unsubscribe from our mailing list via the link in any email.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy when needed. The “Last updated” date will reflect any changes.

9. Contact Us

If you have questions, email us at privacy@canuckdup.ca.
